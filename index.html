Arabic and Islamic Studies Crash Course

Beginner Level (Ibtidaahiyah)

Why Choose This Course?

Unlock the door to classical knowledge! With this crash course, you’ll not only master the intricacies of Arabic but also connect with centuries of Islamic wisdom. This is your opportunity to deepen your faith, sharpen your skills, and engage with the language of the Qur’an.

Overview

This course is tailored for students at the beginning level, combining Arabic language skills and Islamic studies to enhance comprehension and understanding of classical texts and Islamic principles.

Key Features

Delve into basic grammar, syntax, and classical text analysis to refine your Arabic proficiency.

Explore Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, and Aqeedah, connecting Islamic sciences to everyday life.

Master Tajweed rules to recite the Qur'an with accuracy and fluency.

Understand how Arabic serves as the foundation for engaging with Islamic sciences and principles.

Course Duration

This comprehensive crash course spans 1 year, with 4 sessions per week, each lasting 1 hour. Flexible scheduling options are available for your convenience.

Who is This Course For?

Students with a basic foundation in Arabic and Islamic studies who are ready to progress to the intermediate level.

By completing this course, students will enhance their proficiency in Arabic, gain deeper insights into Islamic principles, and build a foundation for advanced studies.
